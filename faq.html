Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Taste of Chi.

How do I submit a restaurant or review?
Use the form on the Contact page. We’ll verify and add it to the site.
Are restaurants verified?
We prioritize community credibility and cross-check with multiple sources. No paid placements.
Do you support dietary filters (halal, vegan, gluten-free)?
Yes — coming to the Restaurants page soon. You’ll be able to filter by dietary needs.
Can I contact the team?
Absolutely. Use the form on the Contact page.

Still need help?

Send us a message on the Contact page — we reply within 2–3 business days.
