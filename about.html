Our Story

Taste of Chi highlights Chicago’s food scene with community recommendations—showcasing real, affordable spots with good quality food.

Why We Exist

Many Food apps tend to focus on chain restaurants, which can make it difficult to find hidden gems or food spots that many may not be familiar with. In addition, tourists who come during the summer months tend to miss out on local favorites, and residents may not know what’s new in their own neighborhood. Therefore, TasteOfChi solves all of these ongoing issues by developing community-based reviews, along with displaying photos to help users find trusted and reliable dining spots.

What We Value

  • Honest, local recommendations
  • Budget-friendly options
  • Simple, accessible design for users
