Our Story

Taste of Chi highlights Chicago’s food scene with community recommendations—showcasing real, affordable spots with good quality food.

Why We Exist

Many food apps focus mainly on chain restaurants, making it hard for users to discover hidden gems or lesser-known local spots. Tourists visiting during the summer often miss out on true neighborhood favorites, and even residents may be unaware of new places opening nearby. TasteOfChi solves these issues by offering community-based reviews and photo sharing, helping users find trusted, reliable, and authentic dining options.

What We Value

  • Honest, local recommendations
  • Budget-friendly options
  • Simple, accessible design for users
