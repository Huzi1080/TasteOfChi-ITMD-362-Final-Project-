Our Story

Taste of Chi celebrates Chicago’s food scene through community recommendations — highlighting authentic, affordable places you might otherwise miss.

Why We Exist

Most discovery apps push chains or sponsored listings. We surface neighborhood favorites suggested by real people, not ads. Our goal is simple: help you eat well, spend less, and experience the city’s real flavor.

What We Value

  • Honest, local recommendations
  • Budget-friendly options
  • Simple, accessible design
